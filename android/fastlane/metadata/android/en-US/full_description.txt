CB Pro VPN is a powerful and user-friendly VPN application designed to provide secure, fast, and reliable internet access with advanced SOCKS5 proxy support.

Key Features:
• Multiple VPN profiles - Save and manage multiple VPN configurations
• SOCKS5 Proxy Support - Direct SOCKS5 connection capability
• Fast & Secure - Encrypted connections for your privacy
• Easy Profile Management - Import/export profiles for quick setup
• Health Checks - Monitor connection status in real-time
• Dark/Light Theme - Customizable interface
• Detailed Logs - Track connection history and troubleshoot issues

Whether you're protecting your privacy, accessing restricted content, or managing multiple proxy configurations, CB Pro VPN provides the tools you need.

Privacy & Security:
We take your privacy seriously. CB Pro VPN does not log your activity or collect personal information.

Requirements:
- Android 10 (API 29) or higher
- Internet permission for VPN functionality
